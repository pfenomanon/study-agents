Answer: Wind damage to the roof is covered under HO-3 only if the TWIA endorsement is active; otherwise, it is excluded. The homeowner should report the damage within 24 hours.

Rationale: The HO-3 policy covers direct physical loss unless specifically excluded, and windstorm damage is excluded unless the TWIA endorsement is in place. Immediate reporting is crucial to comply with the claim notice requirement.

Citations: Professional knowledge, Texas Department of Insurance guidelines.
