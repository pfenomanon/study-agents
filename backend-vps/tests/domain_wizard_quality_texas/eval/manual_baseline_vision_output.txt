Answer: Wind damage to the roof is covered under HO-3 if the TWIA endorsement is active; otherwise, it is excluded. The homeowner must notify the insurer within 24 hours of discovering the damage.

Rationale: The HO-3 policy covers direct physical loss unless specifically excluded, and windstorm damage is excluded unless the TWIA endorsement is in place. Immediate notification is required to comply with the policy terms.
