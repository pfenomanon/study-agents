Answer: Wind damage to the roof is covered under HO-3 only if the TWIA endorsement is active; otherwise, it is excluded. The homeowner must notify the insurer within 24 hours of discovering the damage.

Rationale: The HO-3 policy generally covers direct physical loss unless specifically excluded, and windstorm damage is excluded unless the TWIA endorsement is in place. Immediate notification is crucial for timely claims processing.

Citations: Professional knowledge, Texas Department of Insurance guidelines.
